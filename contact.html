04Common Questions
Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with Phantom Shield.

How quickly can you start a security assessment?

For standard assessments, we can typically begin within 1–2 weeks of scoping. Emergency assessments and incident response engagements can start within 24 hours. We'll work with you to find a timeline that fits your needs.

Do you work with small businesses or only enterprises?

We work with organizations of all sizes. Our Small Business packages start at $499/month and include essential protections. Enterprise engagements are custom-scoped. Every organization deserves professional security.

What compliance frameworks do you support?

We support HIPAA, PCI-DSS, SOC 2, ISO 27001, NIST CSF, and regional frameworks including Panama's Ley 81 and SBP regulations. Our team holds active certifications in all major frameworks.

Do you provide ongoing monitoring or just one-time assessments?

Both. We offer project-based engagements like penetration tests and audits, as well as managed security services with 24/7 monitoring, threat detection, and quarterly reassessments for continuous protection.

Is the initial consultation really free?

Yes. We offer a free 30-minute consultation to understand your environment, identify high-priority risks, and recommend next steps — with zero obligation. We believe you should know what you're buying before you invest.

Can you help if we've already been breached?

Absolutely. Our incident response team is available 24/7. Call our emergency line and we'll begin triage within 15 minutes. We handle containment, forensics, recovery, and post-incident hardening.
