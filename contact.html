Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I submit the form?

▼
After you submit your information, our team will review your request within 24 hours. We'll contact you via your preferred method to understand your specific needs, discuss available services, and provide a customized quote or security assessment timeline. For emergency breach inquiries, we'll contact you immediately.

Do you offer free assessments?

▼
Yes! We offer a free 30-minute consultation to discuss your security concerns and recommend services that fit your organization's needs and budget. This consultation allows us to understand your environment before proposing any paid services. For organizations interested in deeper assessment, we offer flexible pricing options including starter packages for smaller businesses.

What languages do you support?

▼
Phantom Shield provides full support in both English and Spanish. Our team is fluent in both languages and can conduct assessments, consultations, and reports in your preferred language. This is especially valuable for our LATAM operations and clients across Latin America and the Caribbean.

How quickly can you respond to an active breach?

▼
Our emergency incident response team can be mobilized within 2 hours of your call. We have 24/7 on-call specialists trained in immediate containment, forensic investigation, and threat mitigation. Simply call our emergency hotline at +1 (407) 555-0147, and we'll guide you through the critical first steps while assembling our response team.
