Top Threat Types Targeting LATAM

🔓 Credential Theft & Account Takeover

Attackers exploit compromised credentials to gain unauthorized access to critical systems. Once inside, they establish persistence and escalate privileges.

  • Phishing campaigns targeting employee credentials
  • Brute force attacks on remote access systems
  • Lateral movement through compromised accounts
  • Data exfiltration through legitimate access

💰 Ransomware Attacks

Ransomware is the primary threat in LATAM, with organizations facing critical downtime and extortion demands. Costa Rica declared a state of emergency in 2022 due to ransomware attacks.

  • Double extortion tactics (encryption + data theft)
  • Targeting government, healthcare, and financial sectors
  • Average ransom demands in millions of dollars
  • Supply chain compromise leading to widespread deployment

📊 Data Extortion & Breaches

Beyond encryption, attackers steal sensitive data and threaten public release unless demands are met. This affects customer privacy, regulatory compliance, and brand reputation.

  • Customer personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Financial and banking records
  • Healthcare patient records (HIPAA violations)
  • Intellectual property and trade secrets

Critical Sectors Under Attack

🏛️ Government & Public Administration

Government agencies are high-value targets for ransomware, espionage, and disruption attacks. Critical infrastructure depends on secure systems.

🏥 Healthcare Systems

Healthcare providers face patient data theft, operational disruption, and critical care system attacks. HIPAA compliance is mandatory.

💳 Financial Services & Banking

Banks and financial institutions are prime targets for theft, fraud, and regulatory violations. PCI-DSS compliance is critical.

📡 Telecommunications

Telecom companies face infrastructure attacks, customer data breaches, and service disruption threats.

🏭 Manufacturing & Industrial

Industrial control systems, production data, and supply chain systems are increasingly targeted.

Why LATAM Needs Specialized Cybersecurity

Regulatory Complexity / Complejidad Regulatoria

English: Each LATAM country has unique data protection laws, banking regulations, and compliance requirements. Organizations must navigate multiple regulatory frameworks across their operations.

Español: Cada país de LATAM tiene leyes únicas de protección de datos, regulaciones bancarias y requisitos de cumplimiento. Las organizaciones deben navegar múltiples marcos regulatorios en sus operaciones.

Local Threat Intelligence / Inteligencia de Amenazas Local

English: Threat actors targeting LATAM use region-specific attack vectors, social engineering tactics, and infrastructure exploitation methods. Generic global solutions miss these nuances.

Español: Los actores de amenazas dirigidos a LATAM utilizan vectores de ataque específicos de la región, tácticas de ingeniería social y métodos de explotación de infraestructura. Las soluciones globales genéricas pierden estos matices.

Incident Response Readiness / Preparación para Respuesta a Incidentes

English: When breaches occur, response teams must understand local legal requirements, law enforcement procedures, and communication protocols specific to each LATAM country.

Español: Cuando ocurren brechas, los equipos de respuesta deben comprender los requisitos legales locales, los procedimientos de cumplimiento de la ley y los protocolos de comunicación específicos de cada país de LATAM.

Business Continuity in Crisis / Continuidad Empresarial en Crisis

English: LATAM organizations need security strategies that account for infrastructure limitations, economic factors, and regional business practices to ensure practical, affordable protection.

Español: Las organizaciones de LATAM necesitan estrategias de seguridad que tengan en cuenta limitaciones de infraestructura, factores económicos y prácticas comerciales regionales para garantizar protección práctica y asequible.
